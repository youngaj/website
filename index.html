What I'm Doing

I love what I do. I take great pride in what I do. And I can't do something halfway, three-quarters, nine-tenths. If I'm going to do something, I go all the way.

1. Strategy

I've been told that one of my strongest points is my ability to sit with a customer, understand their requirements and offer meaningful suggestions. Key to a good strategy is the collaboration that comes from active listening and empathy.

2. Architecture Design

The best stategy can fail without a good architectual design and execution. I've designed a number of successful systems and even a few unsuccessful systems. With each new deisgn a learn more.

3. Web Design

For customers and end users the user interface (UI) is the application. A great architecture can be overshadowed if the UI and user experience has not been thought out users will not get the value.

4. Group Leadership

I have lead a bi-weekly developer group since late 2012. In order to bootstrap that group I have presented at roughhly 80% of those meetups and mentored several other developers.

5. Development

I am currently developing several applications. My development stack of choice includes JavaScript, Angular.JS, and BootStrap on the front end and ASP.NET (Web API C#), SQL Server and Redis on the server side. I am also working on an application using Node.js and TypeScript

6. Consulting

In my career thus far I have not done any consulting but I am looking for opportunities to expand into new horizons. If you like what you see and think I can help you acheive your short or long term goals please reach out.
